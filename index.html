Event Schedule

Friday 10 Jan. 2020 Saturday 11 Jan. 2020 Sunday 12 Jan. 2020
5:30PM - 6:30PM

Registration, Mingler and Dinner

Meet us at the SE02 second floor's Great Hall open space for registration. Once registered, your IDs and meal tickets will be handed out. Grab some food, kick back and meet the other competitors!

6:30PM - 7:00PM

Welcoming Address

We'll be rallying everyone to do the event introduction. We'll get a scope for why we're putting this event on, and also hear from some of our sponsors who have graciously supported us in our efforts to organize the hackathon.

— Justin Cervantes President, BCIT Student Association

7:00PM - 7:20PM

Event Breakdown

We'll be going over the logistics of the hackathon, from judging criteria to tips and tricks for success.

— Louis Rivest Chair - School of Computing and Academic Studies, BCIT Student Association

7:20PM - 8:30PM

Team Meeting & Adjournment

You'll have a chance to meet your teams, understand each others goals and interests, and discuss what you hope to get out of the event. We're hoping that when you meet again on Saturday morning, you'll be able to hit the ground running!

8:00AM - 9:00AM

Arrival & Registration

We'll be asking attendees to start arriving to meet with their teams and find a place to work. For those participants who were not able to register on the Friday, we will keep the booth open for an hour before the event officially kicks off.

9:00AM - 9:30AM

Challenges Released

We'll be announcing the official challenges and any prizing associated with the best project for a given challenge. We'll have some time for an open Q&A as well. Hacking will begin promptly thereafter.

12:00PM - 1:00PM

Lunch

Lunch will be served during this hour.

6:00PM - 7:00PM

Dinner

Dinner will be served during this hour.

10:00PM - 11:00PM

Late Night Yoga

De-stress, step away from the problem and re-energize. Yoga helps improve circulation and builds self-awareness, its the perfect compliment to a long day of coding.

8:00AM - 9:00AM

Breakfast

Breakfast will be served during this hour.

12:00PM - 1:00PM

Lunch

Lunch will be served during this hour.

1:00PM

Code Submission Deadline

All submissions will be finalized at this time. Any team that has not submitted their documents will be automatically disqualified from any prizing or ranking, however may demo their solutions. All documents must be uploaded to our submission platform, Riipen.

1:00PM - 2:30

Preliminary Judging and Open Expo

At this time judges will go around and begin evaluating teams to determine who the top teams are. While judges are walking around, contestants are encouraged to also visit other team booths to see what great work has been identified!

3:00PM - 4:00

Main Stage Judging

Judging will now begin. We'll call everyone back to the Great Hall and announce the top teams who passed the preliminary round of judging.

4:15

Closing Ceremonies

We'll compile all of the results from the competition and announce the winner of prizes!
